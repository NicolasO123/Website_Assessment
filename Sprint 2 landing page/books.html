The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas is a historical drama film released in 2008, directed by Mark Herman and based on the novel of the same name by John Boyne. The film tells the story of a young German boy named Bruno during World War II, who befriends a Jewish boy named Shmuel after his family moves to the countryside to live near a concentration camp.
