Series Review

Club de Cuervos

"No seas mamón wey.”: Chava Iglesias

Club de Cuervos is a dramedy that follows the story of a wealthy family in Mexico that owns a soccer team, the Cuervos of Nuevo Toledo. The death of the team's owner sets off a power struggle between his two adult children, who are determined to take control of the team and its legacy. The series is known for its mix of humor and drama, as well as its exploration of Mexican politics and culture.

The show has been praised for its strong performances and its representation of Mexican society, including issues of class, race, and gender. It has also been noted for its unique perspective on the world of soccer and the role it plays in Mexican culture.
